Hot and Sour Soup

A warm bowl of hot and sour soup for a chilly day.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tablespoons oil
  • 1 Tablespoon grated ginger
  • 4-5 minced garlic cloves
  • 8 cups vegetable broth
  • 4 oz shiitake mushrooms
  • 8 oz bamboo stalks
  • 14 oz tofu block
  • 1 medium bunch bok choy
  • 2 Tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 Tablespoons Asian chili paste
  • 1 Tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 2 Tablespoons carnstarch mixed with 1/4 cup cold water
  • 6 scallions
  • 1/4 cup cilantro

Directions

  1. Press tofu the night before to drain and then cut into cubes.
  2. Heat 1 Tablespoon of oil in large saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Add ginger and garlic for about 1 minute.
  4. Add broth and raise heat to bring to a boil.
  5. Lower heat and add mushrooms and bamboo, allow to simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a large skillet heat 1 Tablespoon of oil over medium heat.
  7. Brown tofu on each side in skillet and then set aside on a plate.
  8. Stir in bok choy, vinegar, chili, soy sauce, and sugar to saucepan and allow to simmer for 10 minutes.
  9. Add tofu and corn startch mix and remove from heat.
  10. Ladel soup into bowls and garnish with cilantro and scallions.
