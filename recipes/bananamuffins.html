Banana Muffins

A delicious banana muffin fresh out of the oven.

This simple recipe will make delicious muffins you can eat at anytime of the day. The ingredients in this recipe are great for people with digestive issues. Top with honey for a delicous treat!

Ingredients

  • Dry Ingredients
    • 1.5 cups brown rice flour
    • 1/4 cup coconut sugar
    • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
    • 1 teaspoon baking soda
    • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • Wet Ingredients
    • 2 cups mashed ripe bananas (4 to 5 large)
    • 1/2 cup melted coconut oil, plus more for greasing the dish
    • 2 teaspoons raw apple cider vinegar

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 Degrees Farenheit.
  2. Oil a 12-cup muffin tin or line with paper liners.
  3. Whisk together the dry ingredients.
  4. In a separate bowl mix together the wet ingredients.
  5. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry and mix vigorously.
  6. Fill each muffin cup about three quarters full with the batter immediately.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes.
  8. Cool on a wire rack.
